In today's episode we spoke with Dr. Jane Smith about artificial intelligence safety.
She explained that AI safety is one of the most important challenges facing humanity today. The core problem is ensuring that as AI systems become more powerful, they remain aligned with human values and intentions.
Dr. Smith emphasized three key pillars of AI safety: alignment, interpretability, and robustness. Alignment means the AI does what we actually want. Interpretability means we can understand why it makes decisions. Robustness means it behaves well even in unexpected situations.
When asked about timelines, she said significant breakthroughs could happen within the next decade, which is why investment in safety research needs to accelerate now.
